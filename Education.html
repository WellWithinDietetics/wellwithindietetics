General Nutrition Information

This page provides evidence-based nutrition education to support healthier eating habits, improve overall well-being, and guide informed food choices for individuals at all stages of life.

Subject Research Recommendations
Nursing moms Mayo Clinic

CDC Recommendations for nursing moms
  • 330–500 additional calories
  • Focus on protein and healthy fats
  • Prioritize calcium and iron
Endurance Athletes PMC study on rest day nutrition

PMC study on carbohydrates
  • Prioritize complex carbohydrates for fuel and protein for recovery
  • Ensure adequate hydration and replenish minerals with electrolytes
Older adults PMC study on elderly nutrition

AHA article addressing changes in elderly nutrition requirements
  • Prioritize nutrient-dense foods
  • Increase protein and reduce sodium
  • Prioritize fiber
